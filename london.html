Welcome to CityLiving.London

City Living London is a specialist provider of quality contemporary short term serviced apartments located in historic buildings within Central London. Whether you are visiting for business or pleasure, our luxury apartments offer the perfect alternative to a hotel for a short or extended stay. With comprehensive free wifi in all apartments and a regular housekeeping service, you will have the comfort of home without the hassle. Our apartments are perfectly located for both the business hub of the City and the classic tourist sights of the West End, Covent Garden, Bloomsbury and Westminster.
